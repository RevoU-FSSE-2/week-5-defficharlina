Founder and Designer

Diandra Sarasvati, also called Dian, established the company Diandra Sarasvati in 2018. She graduated in 2017 from ESMOD Jakarta’s Fashion Design and Creation Program and earned her master’s degree in 2019 from the Faculty of Fine Arts and Design at the Bandung Institute of Technology. Dian has received numerous honors throughout her career from various local and national contests, including 1st place in International Islamic Fashion & Products of Indonesia 2015, RCTI Hijab Look Fashion Design Competition 2017, YOUC1000 x Esmod Jakarta Fashion Design Competition 2017, and Favorite Winner in Indonesia Fashion Week Fashion Design Competition 2016.
